ABOUT ME

Hello, My name is Zia Ahmad. I currently reside in West Virginia with my wonderful wife and son. The one thing I love most is learning about and building things. Cars, computers, keyboards, watches, and, of course, software, are a few of the things that I’ve built myself.

Most times, I build something because I feel what’s available is lacking something. It could be a function, feature, aesthetic, affordability, or combination of these. A simple example, a two-tone watch with a blue dial. Most manufacturers only pair blue dials with silver watches. So I built my own watch with my choice of dial, case, movement, hands, and bracelet. Why settle for what is available if it’s not what you want or need?

For me, this plays directly into my core values; a unique perspective, determination, and adaptability. I love to look at things differently, determined to find ways to improve them, even if it’s something I’ve never done before. It’s this mindset and these core values that directly influence every application I develop.
